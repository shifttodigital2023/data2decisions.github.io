About Me

Welcome to my biostatistics portfolio! I am a dedicated biostatistician with expertise in statistical analysis, data visualization, and research methodology. My work focuses on analyzing health-related data to drive evidence-based decisions and improve public health outcomes.

With a strong background in statistical programming and research design, I specialize in analyzing complex datasets to extract meaningful insights. My expertise spans clinical trials, epidemiological studies, and health services research.

I am passionate about translating complex statistical concepts into actionable insights for healthcare professionals and researchers. My goal is to contribute to advancements in medical research through rigorous statistical analysis and methodology.
